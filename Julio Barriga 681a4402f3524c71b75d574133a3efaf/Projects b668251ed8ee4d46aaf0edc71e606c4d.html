Muscle-Music with Arduino (Spring 2018)

  • Designed a EMG signal amplifier and filtering circuit using OpAmps, the circuit was integrated by 8 steps:
    1. Instrumentation Amplifier
    1. Low-pass filter
    1. High-pass filter
    1. Inverter Amplifier
    1. Full wave precision rectifier
    1. Passive Low-pass filter
    1. Differential Amplifier
    1. Biased Parallel Clipper
  • Proposed an easy solution using a MyoWare Muscle Sensor.
  • You can check the whole Instructable's tutorial here 👉🏽 Muscle Music

Limb Sensing Ring (Fall 2019)

  • Inspired on the FitSocket project from the Biomechatronics group from MIT MediaLab designed a low-cost version using an Arduino Mega, 4 NEMA17 stepper motors and 3d Printing.
  • Arduino IDE for the data acquisition and MATLAB for plotting the data.
  • Less than $200 USD on materials.
